Write the binding quarterly upstream-sync runbook at `sovereign-shield-backup/documentation/osAgent/UPSTREAM_SYNC.md` per FORK-02. The runbook is the discipline that prevents fork-rot: without it, the fork accumulates conflicts within a year (RESEARCH.md PITFALLS.md Pitfall 7) and the Q4 merge becomes catastrophic.

Output: UPSTREAM_SYNC.md committed to sovereign-shield-backup on a fresh branch with a Touch/Change/Impact/Rollback PR, documentation README cross-linked to the new subdirectory, human-operator approval of the runbook prose before PR merge.
